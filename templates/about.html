About

GraPHOtti is a collaborative, digital Graffiti experience, designed for the WestEndLights, which is one night in the year when the front windows of the houses turn into screens and light up the streets with short films, shadows and light to surprise, delight and enchant local residents and passers-by. It takes place in the Westend of Lincoln, UK, on 4 December 2015.

GraPHOtti allows you to doodle on the front window of 10 Queens Crescent with your smart phone. Just go to https://lcas.lincoln.ac.uk/graphotti/ and doodle away (or scan the QR code or tap your NFC-enabled phone on the tag in front of our house). As this is a community experience, you need to be close to Lincoln's Westend to be able to draw (Hence, your phone will be asking you to allow the website to read your location).

Come to our Window and doodle away with others, share the current Window content on Twitter, follow what others are sharing.
